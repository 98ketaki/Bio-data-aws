BIO-DATA-QC // v0.1

BIO-DATA-QC

Your models deserve better data

02 — Problem

The number one reason AI pilots fail in biotech is data quality

  • Computational biologists spend up to 45% of their time preparing data
  • Critical biological context lives outside structured systems — in protocols, notes, etc.
  • Models are training on it anyway

03 — Landscape

Why existing tools fail

  • Electronic Lab Notebooks (ELNs) capture experiments for Lab Scientists: Benchling, Tetra
  • Lab Information Systems (LIMS) Are Siloed and Inflexible
  • Databases store outputs but are not accessible by Lab scientists
  • Validators check schemas but none of them reason about whether the data is scientifically meaningful
  • No Standardization Enforcement

04 — Solution

BIO-DATA-QC IS A QUALITY AWARE FEATURE STORE

  • QC score travels with every feature
  • Bad data is quarantined before it ever becomes a feature
  • Quality is queryable: filter by score, lot, batch, or instrument at query time
  • Schema is versioned like code — no silent mixing of old models and new data

05 — Customers

Customers

  • Computational Biologists / Bioinformaticians
  • ML/AI team at Biotech and Big Pharma
  • Contract Research Organizations (CROs) generating schema-specific data for each customer
